How to make a Delicious Pepperoni Piza

Pizza is best known for it's pulled cheese and crispy pepperoni's. It is a dish that originated in Italy and is popular all over the world. Today I will give you the steps to create your own delicious pizza!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Required Steps

  1. Place dough on a pizza plate
  2. Add tomato sauce to the dough
  3. Cover the entire pizza shape with Mozarella cheese
  4. Place pepperoni's throughout the pizza
  5. Insert pizza in the oven for 20 minutes
  6. Let it cool for 10 minutes and enjoy!
